The Origin

Born from the exhaustion of the infinite feed. We are re-engineering the web for humans, not engagement metrics.

Our Manifesto

We believe technology took a wrong turn when it began to demand our attention rather than serve our intentions. We are here to reverse that.

Disinvented is a collective of designers, poets, and engineers who are nostalgic for a future that never happened—one where technology amplified our humanity instead of replacing it.

Method

We work backward. We start with the physical sensation of an interaction and write the code to match it.
